Privacy Policy for RETRO GAMES

RETRO GAMES is a web-based mini-game hub. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the site, why it is processed, how long it may be retained, and what choices you have.

Important: This site may display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to deliver, limit, and measure advertisements. See the Cookie/Analysis Guide for details and options.

1) What information we may process

A. Technical / Log Data

When you visit the site, servers and browsers may automatically generate technical data such as access time, requested pages, device/browser type (User-Agent), and approximate IP-based location (country/region level). This is commonly used for security and service stability.

B. Cookies / Similar Technologies

The site may use cookies, local storage (localStorage), and similar identifiers to remember preferences, improve UX, and support advertising/measurement through third-party providers.

2) What we do NOT intentionally collect

  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal data (e.g., health, biometric, political opinions).
  • The game site is not designed to request government-issued IDs or payment information.

3) Purposes of processing

  • Service delivery: deliver pages, run game features, and provide core functionality.
  • Security & reliability: prevent abuse, troubleshoot errors, and protect the service.
  • Analytics (optional): understand traffic and performance to improve UX (may be consent-based depending on configuration).
  • Advertising: show and measure ads (e.g., Google AdSense) to support operating costs.

4) Third-party services (ads/analytics)

RETRO GAMES may use third-party services such as Google AdSense and (optionally) analytics tools. These providers may process data according to their own policies.

  • Google advertising technology: Google Ads Technology
  • Google Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • Manage ad personalization: Google Ad Settings

5) Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes described above. Logs and security records are typically kept for a limited period (for example, 30–180 days) depending on operational needs. Third-party cookie retention varies by provider.

6) Your choices and controls

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can manage ad personalization via Google Ad Settings.
  • If cookie consent UI is enabled, you can accept/reject analytics cookies from the banner/settings.

This site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child’s information has been collected, contact us and we will address it promptly.
