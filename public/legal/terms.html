Privacy Policy for RETRO GAMES

This Privacy Policy explains how RETRO GAMES (“we”, “our”, “the Service”) may handle information when you visit and use our web-based mini games. We aim to minimize data and only use what is needed to operate, secure, and improve the Service.

Important: We may display ads via Google AdSense or other partners. Those partners may use cookies or similar technologies to deliver and measure ads. Please review the Cookies/Analysis Guide for details.

1) Information we may process

Service Operation

Technical logs such as basic request/response data (e.g., timestamp, IP in part or full depending on hosting), browser/OS info (User-Agent), and error events may be processed for security and stability.

Cookies & Similar Tech

We may use cookies / local storage to remember settings and to support analytics/ads. See Cookies/Analysis Guide.

2) What we do NOT intentionally collect

  • We do not ask for sensitive personal data (e.g., resident registration number, health data).
  • We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13.

3) Purpose of processing

  • Operate the Service (game pages, settings, basic functionality).
  • Secure the Service (fraud prevention, abuse response, incident investigation).
  • Improve performance and UX (anonymous statistics, bug fixes).
  • Support ads that help fund the Service (ad delivery/measurement).

4) Third-party services (Ads / Analytics)

We may use third-party providers such as Google for ads (AdSense) and, if enabled, analytics. These providers may process information on their own systems under their policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Technology
  • Google Ad Settings

5) Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above. Security and operational logs may be kept for a limited period (e.g., days to months) depending on hosting and incident needs. Third-party cookies/identifiers follow the provider’s retention policies.

6) Your choices

  • Manage cookies via the site consent UI (if shown) or your browser settings.
  • Control personalized ads in Google Ad Settings.
  • Note: blocking cookies may limit certain features and may show non-personalized ads.
