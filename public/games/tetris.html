GAME RULE

RETRO TETRIS is a classic block puzzle game. Place falling pieces to complete full lines. Clear lines to score points and survive as the game gets faster.

Controls

  • Pause / Resume: Space or U

How to Score

  • Clearing lines increases your score. More lines at once = bigger reward.
  • Combos (clearing lines consecutively) add extra bonuses.
  • Advanced clears (like T-Spin in some situations) may award additional points.
  • Your Best score is saved locally in your browser.

Tips

  • Use Hold (C) to save a piece for a better moment.
  • Keep your stack low and flat to avoid topping out.
  • Plan rotations early and use walls to your advantage.
  • Pause if you need a break (Space/U).
